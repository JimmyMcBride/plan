# Roadmap and Portfolio Planning

Created: 2026-04-16T05:33:06Z

## Outcome

Turn `ROADMAP.md` into a first-class planning surface for versions, ordering, and parked work.

## Why Now

Once the core workflow exists, users need a lightweight way to see how epics roll up into versions without adopting a full PM system.

## Scope Boundary

- roadmap editing workflows
- version and milestone structure
- epic ordering and parking-lot management
- summaries of what each version delivers

Not in scope:

- dependency graph execution logic
- external project management sync
- hosted dashboards
