# Power-User Local Workflows

Created: 2026-04-16T05:33:06Z

## Outcome

Support advanced local workflows for bigger repos and small teams without abandoning the simple core model.

## Why Now

After `plan` has a stable core, roadmap rigor, and dependency logic, the next leverage point is making advanced local workflows feel deliberate instead of improvised.

## Scope Boundary

- richer local status and workflow helpers
- conventions for branch-aware planning usage
- stronger support for multi-epic and multi-version work in one repo

Not in scope:

- hosted collaboration
- enterprise role and permission systems
- external PM integrations
